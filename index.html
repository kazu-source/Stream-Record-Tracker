Common Questions

Why does it say "No ranked games yet"?

▼

You haven't finished any ranked Solo/Duo games since the stream started. Flex, normals, and ARAM don't count.

Why does LP show "N/A"?

▼

LP tracking works best when !record is used before your first game. Set up an auto-trigger when your stream starts!

What if my stream crashes?

▼

No worries! If you restart within 10 minutes, your record continues from where it was.

Can I track Flex queue?

▼

Not yet - currently only Solo/Duo is tracked.

Does it work for TFT or VALORANT?

▼

Yes! It will auto-detect if you set your Twitch category to League of Legends, TFT, or VALORANT.
