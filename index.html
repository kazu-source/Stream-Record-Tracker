You're All Set!

Type !record in your chat to see your stream's ranked record. It tracks:

  • Wins and losses this stream
  • Total LP gained or lost
  • Only ranked Solo/Duo games
Note

Games take a few minutes to appear after they finish. The Riot API needs time to update!

Common Questions

Why does it say "No ranked games yet"?

▼

You haven't finished any ranked Solo/Duo games since the stream started. Flex, normals, and ARAM don't count.

Why does LP show "N/A"?

▼

LP tracking works best when !record is used before your first game. Set up an auto-trigger when your stream starts!

What if my stream crashes?

▼

No worries! If you restart within 10 minutes, your record continues from where it was.

Can I track Flex queue?

▼

Not yet - currently only Solo/Duo is tracked. Flex support is planned for a future update.

Does it work for TFT?

▼

Yes! Add &game=tft to your command URL, or it will auto-detect if you set your Twitch category to TFT.
